## Bug ID: 001

Date: 2026-03-05
Buggy Sentence: I was not seeing him since 2015.
Correct Sentence: I haven't seen him since 2015.
Root Cause: Confused Past Continuous with Present Perfect — "since" requires Perfect tense
Rule: "Since" always triggers Present Perfect (or Past Perfect in reported speech)
Category: Tense
Times Repeated: 1

## Bug ID: 002

Date: 2026-03-05
Buggy Sentence: She suggested to go to the cinema.
Correct Sentence: She suggested going to the cinema.
Root Cause: "suggest" belongs to Verb + -ing group, never to-infinitive
Rule: suggest / enjoy / avoid / mind / consider / finish → always + -ing
Category: Gerund-Infinitive
Times Repeated: 1

---

## Bug ID: 003

Date: 2026-03-05
Buggy Sentence: John said he will come tomorrow.
Correct Sentence: John said he would come.
Root Cause: Forgot tense backshift in Reported Speech (will → would)
Rule: In reported speech, present tense shifts one step back into the past
Category: Tense
Times Repeated: 1

---

## Bug ID: 004

Date: 2026-03-05
Buggy Sentence: There is a lack of agreetion.
Correct Sentence: There is a lack of agreement.
Root Cause: Wrong word form — AGREE → noun must be "agreement" not "agreetion"
Rule: agree (v) → agreement (n) | Use VIU-Upper Unit 1–6 for suffix patterns
Category: Word Form
Times Repeated: 1

---

## Bug ID: 005

Date: 2026-03-05
Buggy Sentence: If I was you, I would accept the offer.
Correct Sentence: If I were you, I would accept the offer.
Root Cause: Type 2 conditional — "be" always uses "were" for all subjects (subjunctive mood)
Rule: If I/he/she/it WERE (not "was") in hypothetical/unreal conditions
Category: Conditional
Times Repeated: 1
