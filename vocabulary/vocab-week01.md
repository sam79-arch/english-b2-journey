# Week 01 - Pre-Intermediate Vocabulary

**Date:** 2026-03-05 to 2026-03-11  
**Source:** Vocabulary In Use Pre-Intermediate (VIU-Pre)

## Day 01: IT Context & Technology (2026-03-05)

| Word / Collocation | Word Form | Meaning (Vietnamese/English) | Example Sentence |
|---|---|---|---|
| In charge of | (phrase) | Phụ trách, chịu trách nhiệm | I am in charge of the IT department. |
| Responsible for | (phrase) | Chịu trách nhiệm về (việc gì đó) | Who is responsible for updating the website? |
| Deal with | (phrasal verb) | Giải quyết, xử lý (khách hàng, lỗi) | I deal with customer complaints every day. |
| Go out of order | (phrase) | Hỏng hóc (máy móc, hệ thống) | The printer has gone out of order again. |
| Sort out | (phrasal verb) | Giải quyết vấn đề (problem) | We need to sort out this bug before launch. |
| Attach (a file) | (v) | Đính kèm (vào email, tài liệu) | I attached the report to the email. |
| Look up | (phrasal verb) | Tra cứu thông tin (từ điển, mạng) | If I don't know a word, I look it up. |
| Back up (files) | (phrasal verb) | Sao lưu dữ liệu | You must back up your files regularly. |
| Crash | (v) | Sập nguồn, lỗi màn hình xanh (hệ thống) | My computer crashed while I was working. |
| Connect to | (v) | Kết nối (mạng, thiết bị) | I can't connect to the Wi-Fi network. |
| Attend (a meeting) | (v) | Tham gia cuộc họp | I have to attend a meeting at 10 a.m. |
| Present (a report) | (v) | Báo cáo, thuyết trình | He presented the sales report very well. |
| Meet a deadline | (phrase) | Hoàn thành đúng hạn | We had to work late to meet the deadline. |
| Set up | (phrasal verb) | Cài đặt, thiết lập hệ thống | The IT guy comes to set up the new software. |
| Upgrade | (v/n) | Nâng cấp | We need to upgrade the database servers. |
