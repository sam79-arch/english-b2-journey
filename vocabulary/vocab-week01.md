# Week 01 - Pre-Intermediate Vocabulary

**Date:** 2026-03-05 to 2026-03-11  
**Source:** Vocabulary In Use Pre-Intermediate (VIU-Pre)

## Day 01: IT Context & Technology (2026-03-05)

### 1. In charge of *(phrase)*  
**Meaning:** Phụ trách, chịu trách nhiệm  
**Example:** *I am in charge of the IT department.*  

### 2. Responsible for *(phrase)*  
**Meaning:** Chịu trách nhiệm về (việc gì đó)  
**Example:** *Who is responsible for updating the website?*  

### 3. Deal with *(phrasal verb)*  
**Meaning:** Giải quyết, xử lý (khách hàng, lỗi)  
**Example:** *I deal with customer complaints every day.*  

### 4. Go out of order *(phrase)*  
**Meaning:** Hỏng hóc (máy móc, hệ thống)  
**Example:** *The printer has gone out of order again.*  

### 5. Sort out *(phrasal verb)*  
**Meaning:** Giải quyết vấn đề (problem)  
**Example:** *We need to sort out this bug before launch.*  

### 6. Attach *(v)*  
**Meaning:** Đính kèm (vào email, tài liệu)  
**Example:** *I attached the report to the email.*  

### 7. Look up *(phrasal verb)*  
**Meaning:** Tra cứu thông tin (từ điển, mạng)  
**Example:** *If I don't know a word, I look it up.*  

### 8. Back up *(phrasal verb)*  
**Meaning:** Sao lưu dữ liệu (files)  
**Example:** *You must back up your files regularly.*  

### 9. Crash *(v)*  
**Meaning:** Sập nguồn, lỗi màn hình xanh (hệ thống)  
**Example:** *My computer crashed while I was working.*  

### 10. Connect to *(v)*  
**Meaning:** Kết nối (mạng, thiết bị)  
**Example:** *I can't connect to the Wi-Fi network.*  

### 11. Attend *(v)*  
**Meaning:** Tham gia (cuộc họp)  
**Example:** *I have to attend a meeting at 10 a.m.*  

### 12. Present *(v)*  
**Meaning:** Báo cáo, thuyết trình (một báo cáo)  
**Example:** *He presented the sales report very well.*  

### 13. Meet a deadline *(phrase)*  
**Meaning:** Hoàn thành đúng hạn  
**Example:** *We had to work late to meet the deadline.*  

### 14. Set up *(phrasal verb)*  
**Meaning:** Cài đặt, thiết lập hệ thống  
**Example:** *The IT guy comes to set up the new software.*  

### 15. Upgrade *(v/n)*  
**Meaning:** Nâng cấp  
**Example:** *We need to upgrade the database servers.*
