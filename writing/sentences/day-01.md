# Day 01 – Sentence Practice

Date: 2026-03-05
Grammar Focus: Present Simple vs Present Continuous (GIU Unit 1)
Vocabulary Focus: deploy, implement, maintain

Sentences:
1. I'm working right now. (Simple and correct!)
2. Look! The girl is eating. (Capitalization fix).
3. I'm currently working on a new JavaScript project. (Article 'a' and capitalization fix).
4. I'm having great sex at the moment. (Correction: No 'a' before 'sex' in this context).
5. I'm eating potato chips at present. (Correction: Use plural 'chips' or 'a potato chip').
6. I am in charge of the IT department and I deal with customer complaints every day. (Vocabulary: in charge of, deal with).
7. We are sorting out a critical bug because the system is crashing. (Vocabulary: sort out, crash).

Reflection:
What grammar mistakes appeared today?
- Small mistakes in capitalization (i -> I) and missing articles (a new project).
- Confusing Present Simple/Continuous for temporary vs permanent states (checked in GIU Units 1 & 2).

What will I focus on tomorrow?
- Units 3 & 4 of GIU (Present Perfect).
- More IT vocabulary from VIU.
