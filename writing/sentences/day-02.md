# Day 02 - Writing Practice (2026-03-06)

## Context: IT & Personal
Focus: Present Simple vs. Present Continuous (Unit 3 & 4)

---

### Sentences
1. **Original:** I cant understand why he's being so stupid. He isn't usually like that
   **Corrected:** I **can't** understand why **he's being** so stupid. He isn't usually like that.
   *Note: Correct use of "he's being" (Unit 4) and capitalization.*

2. **Original:** im love to go fishsing
   **Corrected:** **I love** to go **fishing**.
   *Note: "Love" is a stative verb (Unit 4), no need for "am".*

3. **Original:** im often feel exhausted in the morning
   **Corrected:** **I often feel** exhausted in the morning.
   *Note: Use Present Simple for daily habits (Unit 3).*

4. **Original:** im think Huong not a good person
   **Corrected:** **I think** Huong **is not** a good person.
   *Note: "Think" as an opinion is stative. Added "is" for subject Huong.*

5. **Original:** im want to hit the person who do silent treatment to me
   **Corrected:** **I want** to hit the person who **gives** me the silent treatment.
   *Note: "Want" is stative. Collocation: "give someone the silent treatment".*
