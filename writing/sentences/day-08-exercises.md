## Key Focus Areas for Day 08

### ⚠️ Stative Verbs (Unit 4) — PRIORITY

These verbs NEVER use continuous form:
- **Emotions:** like, love, hate, prefer, enjoy
- **Thinking:** know, understand, believe, think, realize
- **Possession:** have, own, belong
- **Perception:** see, hear, smell, taste, feel
- **Other:** want, need, feel, suit, depend

**WRONG:** "I am knowing the answer"  
**CORRECT:** "I know the answer"

---

### Present Continuous Signals (Unit 1, Unit 3)

Use when:
- **NOW** (right now, at this moment, at this second)
- **TEMPORARY** (for a short time, not permanent)
- **CHANGING** (process happening)

**Example:**
- "I am working on this bug **right now**" ✅
- "She is living in Hanoi **for 2 years**" ❌ → Should be "She lives in Hanoi"

---

### Present Simple Signals (Unit 2)

Use when:
- **GENERAL TRUTH** (all the time, usually, always)
- **PERMANENT** (lasting long time)
- **HABIT/ROUTINE** (every day, weekly, etc.)
- **STATIVE VERBS** (emotions, thoughts, possession)
