## Key Focus Areas for Day 09

### **Unit 5: Past Simple (I did)** 🟨

Use for:
- **Completed action** in the past (finished, not ongoing)
- **Specific time** (yesterday, last week, at 3 PM, in 2020)
- **Series of past actions**

**Examples:**
- "I **finished** the project yesterday" ✅
- "She **called** me last night" ✅
- "They **launched** the product last month" ✅

---

### **Unit 6: Past Continuous (I was doing)** 🟨

Use for:
- **Process in the past** (not complete, still ongoing)
- **Action interrupted** by another action
- **Specific moment** in the past (at 3 PM)

**Examples:**
- "I **was working** when you called" ✅ (process interrupted)
- "What **were** you **doing** yesterday?" ✅ (process at specific time)
- "While she **was reading**, I **was preparing**" ✅ (two ongoing processes)

---

### **Units 5 + 6 Together** 🎯

Pattern: **While + Past Continuous, + Past Simple**

**WRONG:** "While I was working, he was calling"  
**CORRECT:** "While I was working, he called" ✅
