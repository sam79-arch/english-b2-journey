# Day 04 - Writing Practice (2026-03-08) — Accelerated (Units 7-10)

## Context: IT & Personal
Focus: Present Perfect Simple vs. Continuous

---

### Part 1: Present Perfect Simple (Units 7 & 8)
Focus: Result now, Experiences.

1. **Original:** I have finished my work.
   **Corrected:** I **have finished** my work.
   *Note:* Correct use as a result at present.

2. **Original:** She has bought a new laptop.
   **Corrected:** She **has bought** a new laptop.
   *Note:* Good use for a recent action.

3. **Original:** They have just arrived at the office.
   **Corrected:** They **have just arrived** at the office.
   *Note:* Correct position of "just".

4. **Original:** He has never eaten sushi before.
   **Corrected:** He **has never eaten** sushi before.
   *Note:* Perfect for expressing experience.

5. **Original:** We have already sent the email.
   **Corrected:** We **have already sent** the email.
   *Note:* Correct use of "already" for completion.

6. **Original:** Have you seen this movie yet?
   **Corrected:** **Have you seen** this movie **yet**?
   *Note:* Correct question form and position of "yet".

7. **Original:** I have lost my phone.
   **Corrected:** I **have lost** my phone.
   *Note:* Classic present perfect (result: I don't have it now).

8. **Original:** She has lived in Ho Chi Minh City for 5 years.
   **Corrected:** She **has lived** in Ho Chi Minh City for 5 years.
   *Note:* Correct use of "for" for duration.

9. **Original:** He has broken his leg.
   **Corrected:** He **has broken** his leg.
   *Note:* Result in the present (the leg is currently broken).

10. **Original:** I have never been to Japan.
    **Corrected:** I **have never been to** Japan.
    *Note:* Excellent use of "been to" (meaning you went and came back).

---
> **💡 Section D Clarification (GIU): gone to vs been to**
> - **gone to**: Đang ở đó / chưa về. (*He has gone to Japan* = Anh ấy đang ở Nhật).
> - **been to**: Đã đi và đã về. (*He has been to Japan* = Anh ấy từng đi Nhật rồi).
