# Day 03 - Writing Practice (2026-03-07)

## Context: IT & Personal
Focus: Past Simple vs. Past Continuous (Unit 5 & 6)

---

### Sentences

#### 🕒 Past Simple (Quá khứ Đơn)
1. **Original:** I fixed a critical bug in the payment module yesterday.
   **Corrected:** I **fixed** a critical bug in the payment module yesterday.
   *Note:* Perfect use of "yesterday" with Past Simple.

2. **Original:** The server crashed at midnight and we lost all the data.
   **Corrected:** The server **crashed** at midnight and we **lost** all the data.
   *Note:* Correct sequence of finished actions.

3. **Original:** She graduated from university in 2020 and joined our team right after.
   **Corrected:** She **graduated** from university in 2020 and **joined** our team right after.
   *Note:* Good use of "in 2020".

4. **Original:** He didn't attend the meeting because he was sick.
   **Corrected:** He **didn't attend** the meeting because he **was** sick.
   *Note:* Correct negative form and "be" in the past.

5. **Original:** Did you deploy the new version last Friday?
   **Corrected:** **Did you deploy** the new version last Friday?
   *Note:* Correct question form with "Did".

#### 🕒 Past Continuous (Quá khứ Tiếp diễn)
1. **Original:** I was reviewing the pull request when my laptop suddenly died.
   **Corrected:** I **was reviewing** the pull request when my laptop suddenly **died**.
   *Note:* Excellent "Interruption" (Quy tắc cắt ngang).

2. **Original:** She was working on the new feature at midnight when the client called.
   **Corrected:** She **was working** on the new feature at midnight when the client **called**.
   *Note:* Accurate use of "at midnight" for progress.

3. **Original:** We were having a team meeting when the power went out.
   **Corrected:** We **were having** a team meeting when the power **went** out.
   *Note:* Another great example of an interruption.

4. **Original:** He wasn't paying attention during the code review, so he missed the bug.
   **Corrected:** He **wasn't paying** attention during the code review, so he **missed** the bug.
   *Note:* Correct negative continuous form.

5. **Original:** What were you doing when the deployment failed?
   **Corrected:** What **were you doing** when the deployment **failed**?
   *Note:* Perfect question form in Past Continuous.
